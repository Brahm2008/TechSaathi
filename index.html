Built for Growth

TechSaathi is being developed as a practice-first digital literacy platform focused on everyday online tasks that often create confusion — especially forms, uploads, payments, account access and safe browsing.

Future versions can expand into ticket booking, government service workflows, bill payments, QR code awareness and other real-world digital tasks based on user research and community needs.
